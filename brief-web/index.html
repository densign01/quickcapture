About Brief

Brief lets you save and summarize articles with clean, simple email delivery and optional AI-generated summaries.

How to Use

1. Paste an article URL
2. Click "Analyze Article"
3. Enter your email address
4. Optionally add a note and enable AI summary
5. Click "Send to Email"

Privacy

Your email address is only used to send you the article summary and is not stored or shared.
