Brief - Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2024

Overview

Brief is a simple app that helps you save web articles to your email. We respect your privacy and collect only what's necessary to provide the service.

Information We Collect

When you use Brief, we collect:

  • Email address: The email address you provide to receive saved articles
  • URLs and titles: The web pages you choose to share through the app
  • Optional notes: Any context you add when saving an article

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Send saved articles to your email address
  • Generate AI summaries when you enable this feature

Data Storage

  • Your email address is stored locally on your device
  • Article data is processed through our API and not permanently stored
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

Third-Party Services

Brief uses the following services:

  • Cloudflare Workers: To process API requests
  • Anthropic Claude: To generate article summaries (when enabled)
  • Resend: To deliver emails

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your email from the app at any time
  • Disable AI summaries in settings
  • Stop using the service at any time

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@danielensign.com

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of Brief constitutes acceptance of any changes.
